Overview
KickSphere is a next-generation football streaming and analytics platform designed to redefine how fans experience the game. Unlike traditional sports apps, it combines live streaming, real-time statistics, and interactive insights in one unified experience.

The project focuses on delivering a seamless connection between live sports and data visualization. Every interaction is built to feel fast, immersive, and emotionally engaging.

The design system emphasizes clarity, motion, and depth using a dark UI with neon green and blue accents to highlight live moments and key actions.
⚽ A real-time football experience ecosystem — not just a sports app.
Problem
Traditional football apps focus mainly on scores and basic match updates, offering a passive experience that lacks engagement and real-time interaction. Fans are disconnected from deeper match insights such as live statistics, tactical analysis, and immersive viewing experiences.
Solution
KickSphere transforms the football experience by combining live streaming, real-time statistics, and interactive insights into one unified platform. Users can follow matches dynamically, analyze performance instantly, and engage with a smart, immersive sports ecosystem.

Stakeholder Meeting

At the beginning of the project, a stakeholder meeting was conducted to align the product vision with business goals and user expectations. The discussion focused on how users interact with football applications during live matches and news tracking.

We explored key features such as live streaming, real-time statistics, and match insights to identify what truly matters to users.

Questions for Discussion:

  • What core features are essential for the app?
  • How can we improve real-time interaction?
  • What information do users prioritize?
  • What are the main pain points in current apps?
  • How can we increase engagement?
