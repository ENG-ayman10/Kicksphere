Overview
        KickSphere is a next-generation football streaming and analytics platform designed to redefine how fans experience the game.
        Unlike traditional sports apps, it combines live streaming, real-time statistics, and interactive insights in one unified experience.
        The project focuses on delivering a seamless connection between live sports and data visualization. Every interaction is built to feel fast,
        immersive, and emotionally engaging.
        The design system emphasizes clarity, motion, and depth using a dark UI with neon green and blue accents to highlight live moments and key actions.
        ⚽ A real-time football experience ecosystem — not just a sports app.
Problem
          Traditional football apps focus mainly on scores and basic match updates, 
          offering a passive experience that lacks engagement and real-time interaction.
          Fans are disconnected from deeper match insights such as live statistics, 
          tactical analysis, and immersive viewing experiences.
Solution
          KickSphere transforms the football experience by combining live streaming, 
          real-time statistics, and interactive insights into one unified platform.
          Users can follow matches dynamically, analyze performance instantly, 
          and engage with a smart, immersive sports ecosystem.
